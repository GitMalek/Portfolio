About Me

This portfolio is best viewed on desktop.

Hi! I'm Malek, 26 years old, Egyptian. I'm currently in my last year of a management degree at the German University in Cairo. I'm an aspiring indie developer, currently trying to be effective at solo-development to improve my skills enough to attract a team.

I've been interested in game development long since I was in school, but the path to putting the interest into practice sadly wasn't direct. I started university in engineering since I hoped it would carry over to development, but my father passed away my first semester, and my uncle the next during covid. I came back from that slower than I'd like to admit, and ended up in a management degree instead.

I mistook management for an 'easier' path while I recovered, but I'd realized that there was no substitute for passion. Even through my studies, my thesis was games industry focused- and I dedicated my personal time to learning multiple development skills.

Game Development

I took a summer in sweden, at the game development incubator Spelkollektivet- voluntarily following their "Indie Quest" self-driven course. I had also joined my university's game development club, winning in two categories for their game jam in my first semester with them and helping them design their art curriculum for the next.

I've been building since then with varied focuses. I've joined a few game jams as an artist, and have built a few prototypes- landing on my current passion project 'From the Hearth'. I've worked on side-projects such an ace attorney-like mystery game where you have to personally deduce the suspect and choose to pressure them, but From The Hearth's design has a lot to say- and I hoped it would sufficiently display how I handle all sorts of design challenges!

The portfolio may show that I'm pretty multi-disciplinary, but I assure you- all the skills I've learned are in service of design! My goal in learning art and coding is to be able to interface better with artists and programmers, and to build my design decisions off experience in every involved field.
